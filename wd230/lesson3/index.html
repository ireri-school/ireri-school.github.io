Site Name

BOOM! Condensation is the site name. The domain is available as boomcondensation.com. You can find it on Google Domains here.

Site Purpose

The site purpose is to bring people of the same community together by providing a space to not only check the weather for their area/region, but also a space to share local news, photos of nature and buildings within the region, and events.
