Audience

The audience for this site would be anyone in the area or looking to move to the area who would like to learn more about the surrounding weather, people, and local events.

Persona #1

-Demographics:

  • Fictional Name: Lily James
  • Age: 33
  • Gender: Female
  • Occupation: Unemployed; Stay at home mother of 3

-Scenarios:

  1. Looks to see what the weather will be like to know what her kids should wear/bring to school for the day.
  2. Looks for local children/family events for weekend fun.
  3. Posts a question on the local forum about where to find a particular type of vacuum.
  4. Reads an article about the best schools in the region for her kids.
  5. Looks to join a local group/club of other mothers her age for socializing.

Persona #2:

-Demographics

  • Fictional Name: Hank Green
  • Age: 20
  • Gender: Male
  • Occupation: Part-time Delivery Driver; Part-time College Student

-Scenarios

  1. Hank delivers local goods within the region for work daily, and needs to keep tabs on the weather to make sure he takes the best routes.
  2. Looks over the news to avoid roads with lots of traffic.
  3. Hank likes to go on hikes, so he checks the weather for the coming weekend to see what he should wear for the hike, or if weather conditions would be too dangerous for a hike.
  4. Finds an article with a list of the best hiking trails in the area.
  5. Checks the weather every morning before going to school.
